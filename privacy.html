Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2025

Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the service:

  • Email Address: Collected via Supabase Auth (for Free tier) or Gumroad (for Professional tier) to manage your account and license.
  • License Keys: To validate your purchase and subscription tier.
  • Device Fingerprints: Anonymized identifiers used to enforce activation limits (e.g., max 3 devices for Professional users).

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the application.
  • To validate your license key and ensure compliance with tier limits.
  • To prevent abuse and enforce activation limits across devices.
  • To communicate important service updates (we do not send marketing spam).

Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party providers for core functionality:

Supabase

Database & Auth

Gumroad

Payments & Licensing

Google Gemini

AI Analysis (Your API Key)

Note: When using "Cloud AI" mode, font data is sent to Google's Gemini API. When using "Chrome AI" (Local), data stays on your device.

Data Storage

Your account data (email, license) is stored securely in Supabase cloud infrastructure. We do not store your uploaded font files or images permanently; they are processed in memory for analysis and then discarded, unless you explicitly save an analysis to your local history.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Request a copy of your data (Data Portability).
